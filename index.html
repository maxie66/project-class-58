Atheletics

Atheletics is a name for various sports that test speed and strength.A person who takes part in an atheletics competition is called athelete There are two types of atheletic events:track and field.Track events are running and walking races.Field eevents are jumping and throwing contests.Objects thrown by atheletes include hard disk,called a discus,and a light spear,called a javelin.Most track events take place on an ova running track , which is divided into eight lanes.Field events usually take place on a field inside the track. The winner is the sthlete who runs,walks,throws,or jumps the fastest,farthest, or highest.Atheletics events are among the most popular at the olympic games, and they were a major feature of the early Olympics held in Ancient Greece.
